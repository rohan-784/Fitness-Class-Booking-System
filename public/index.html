About

"Hi I'm John from Fit Class, a qualified personal trainer with a passion to help women on their journey to becoming happier, healthier, and stronger. I've continued to pursue this passion my whole life and now have a degree in physical education. I pride myself on teaching both men and women a sustainable approach to health and fitness.
